# Changelog

To add a new entry: copy the template below, prepend it at the top of the list in index.html
under the `<!-- CHANGELOG -->` section. Badge types: ship / fix / idea / kill.

---

## Template

```html
<li class="changelog-item">
  <span class="changelog-date">YYYY-MM-DD</span>
  <div class="changelog-content">
    <div class="changelog-title">
      <span class="changelog-badge badge-ship">Ship</span>
      Entry title here
    </div>
    <div class="changelog-note">One or two sentences. What shipped, what it proved, what it fixed.</div>
  </div>
</li>
```
